# Brand Positioning Guide

**Brand Name:** TheEduAssist
**Tagline:** E-Learning Design Agency

**Positioning:**
TheEduAssist helps coaches, course creators, educators, and training teams design, build, migrate, and launch online learning systems across LMS platforms, course platforms, Kajabi, AI-supported workflows, and digital training systems.

**Shorter brand line:**
Multi-platform eLearning, LMS, and course launch support.

**Main homepage hook:**
Design, Build, and Launch Better Online Learning Systems

**Alternative if layout needs shorter H1:**
Build Better Online Learning Across LMS, Kajabi, and Course Platforms

**Important wording rule:**
Kajabi is one supported platform. Do not make the whole company look Kajabi-only.

**Do not claim:**
* official Kajabi partnership
* guaranteed sales
* guaranteed revenue
* guaranteed ranking
* fake client results
* fake testimonials
* fake platform partnerships

# Content Team Onboarding

This document provides a framework for onboarding content team members to TheEduAssist's new Sanity CMS workflow.

## Team Roles
- **Project Manager (PM):** Oversees the migration process, assigns tasks via the Migration Tracker, and acts as the bridge between writers and developers.
- **Editor:** Reviews submitted drafts for clarity, tone, formatting, and SEO requirements. Approves content before final publishing.
- **Writer:** Researches and writes content directly in Sanity (or external docs before migrating to Sanity). Ensures adherence to brand rules and guidelines.
- **Developer:** Manages the Sanity schemas, handles the website frontend integration, and troubleshoots technical issues.

## 60–90 Minute Onboarding Agenda
1. **Introduction (10 mins):** What is a headless CMS? Why are we moving from WordPress to Sanity + Astro?
2. **Sanity Studio Tour (15 mins):** Logging in, navigating the sidebar, finding different content types (Services, Case Studies, Blogs).
3. **Content Standards & Brand Rules (15 mins):** Reviewing SEO guidelines, naming conventions, NO fake claims policy (no fake metrics, fake testimonials, fake client names, or fake official Kajabi partnerships).
4. **Image Handling (10 mins):** How to upload images, adding Alt text, compressing images before upload, and what *not* to upload.
5. **Workflow & Tracking (10 mins):** How to use the Content Migration Tracker and Image Migration Tracker to pick up assignments and report progress.
6. **Practice Task & Q&A (20-30 mins):** Everyone creates a draft document in Sanity (e.g., the Kajabi Course Setup practice content).

## First Practice Task
Follow the instructions at the end of the `SANITY_BEGINNER_GUIDE.md` to create the "Kajabi Course Setup" service entry. Once created, mark the document as a Draft (do not publish).

## Writer Rules
- Use the brand name strictly as "TheEduAssist".
- Primary CTA is always "Book Free Audit".
- Keep copy professional and practical. No exaggerated claims like "revolutionize learning" or "unleash your potential".
- Do not add fake testimonials or fake metrics.
- Do not add fake platform partnerships (We support Kajabi, Moodle, etc., but we do not claim official partnership).
- Alt text is required for every single image.

## Editor Checklist
- [ ] Is the brand name spelled correctly ("TheEduAssist")?
- [ ] Are the SEO Title and Meta Description filled out?
- [ ] Are all image Alt texts present and descriptive?
- [ ] Are there any fake claims, fake metrics, or fake testimonials? (If yes, reject the draft).
- [ ] Is the tone professional and practical?

## Publishing Workflow
1. **Writer** picks an assignment from the Content Migration Tracker.
2. **Writer** drafts content in Sanity.
3. **Writer** updates their status on the Migration Tracker to "Draft Complete".
4. **Editor** reviews the content in Sanity using the Editor Checklist.
5. **Editor** updates the status to "Approved" or leaves notes for revisions.
6. Once approved, the **Editor** clicks Publish in Sanity.

# Internal Linking Strategy

- Homepage links to core services.
- Service pages link back to `/services/`.
- Kajabi page links to `/book-free-audit/`.
- Blog pages link to relevant service pages.
- Ensure no broken internal links.
- Do not link to old WordPress template/plugin URLs.
- Old blog posts should be updated during migration to link to new Astro routes.
- Avoid linking to redirected URLs internally.
- Internal links should use final clean URLs.

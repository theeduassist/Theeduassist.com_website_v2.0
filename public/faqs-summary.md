# Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

## Kajabi Services

**Q: Do you offer Kajabi migration services?**
A: Yes, we help migrate courses, contacts, and content from platforms like WordPress, Teachable, or Thinkific to Kajabi.

**Q: Can you help set up Kajabi funnels and emails?**
A: Yes, we provide support for setting up Kajabi pipelines, funnels, and email automations to support your course launches.

**Q: What if I just need help uploading my videos and PDFs to Kajabi?**
A: We offer Kajabi course upload and cleanup services to organize your raw materials into structured modules and lessons.

## LMS & Platform Selection

**Q: Which LMS platforms do you work with?**
A: We support implementation and migration for platforms such as Moodle, Tutor LMS, LearnWorlds, Docebo, TalentLMS, and WordPress LMS systems.

**Q: Can you help me choose the right LMS?**
A: Yes. We are platform-agnostic and will guide you to the LMS that best fits your specific audience and business needs.

## Custom eLearning & Instructional Design

**Q: What do I need to provide to start a custom eLearning project?**
A: You can bring raw content, existing presentations (PowerPoint/Keynote), recorded webinars, PDFs, or worksheets. We will help structure it into interactive lessons.

**Q: Do you offer instructional design?**
A: Yes, we provide instructional design and course structuring to ensure your content is effective, engaging, and aligned with adult learning principles.

**Q: Who owns the Intellectual Property (IP) of the courses you build?**
A: The client retains 100% ownership of all course content and IP upon project completion and final payment.

## AI & Ethics Training

**Q: How do you use AI in eLearning development?**
A: We use AI carefully to support course planning, content structuring, generating engagement ideas, and assisting with training workflows.

**Q: Is AI-generated content reviewed?**
A: Yes, human review and quality control are a mandatory part of our process for all AI-supported work to ensure accuracy and brand alignment.

**Q: Do your AI Ethics courses guarantee legal compliance?**
A: No. We provide "AI Literacy" and "Responsible AI Training" based on your company policies. We do not provide legal advice or compliance guarantees.

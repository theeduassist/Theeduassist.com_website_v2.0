# TheEduAssist Case Studies Summary

The following are anonymized examples of projects TheEduAssist has completed for various clients.

## 1. SQA-Aligned Vocational Training
*   **Client Type:** Vocational Training Provider
*   **Challenge:** The client had traditional, paper-based classroom materials that needed to be digitized while strictly adhering to Scottish Qualifications Authority (SQA) standards.
*   **Solution:** We structured and developed comprehensive online learning modules. We transformed the static materials into accessible, interactive eLearning formats that maintained compliance with the required educational standards.
*   **Outcome:** A scalable online program that expanded the provider's reach while maintaining rigorous accreditation requirements.

## 2. Business Analytics for Corporate Teams
*   **Client Type:** Corporate Training Department
*   **Challenge:** The company needed to upskill its workforce in business analytics, but existing training was too theoretical and lacked practical application.
*   **Solution:** We designed and implemented a custom business analytics training program. The course focused heavily on practical application, utilizing software simulations to allow employees to practice in a risk-free environment.
*   **Outcome:** Improved employee competency in utilizing analytics tools for daily decision-making.

## 3. Language Courses for a Book Publisher
*   **Client Type:** Educational Publisher
*   **Challenge:** The publisher wanted to create a new digital revenue stream by adapting their popular traditional language learning textbooks for online learners.
*   **Solution:** We converted the textbook materials into interactive digital courses. This included integrating audio pronunciations, interactive assessments, and engaging practice activities that were not possible in print format.
*   **Outcome:** A successful launch of a digital product line that complemented their physical book sales and engaged a modern audience.

# TheEduAssist Pricing & Engagement Models Summary

TheEduAssist offers flexible engagement models tailored to the scope and scale of different e-learning projects.

## Common Project Types

### 1. Platform Setup & Migrations (e.g., Kajabi)
*   **Scope:** Migrating existing courses, setting up pipelines, designing landing pages, and configuring email automations.
*   **Pricing Approach:** Typically quoted as flat-fee project rates based on the complexity and volume of assets to be migrated/built.

### 2. Custom Course Development
*   **Scope:** Transforming raw materials into structured modules, storyboarding, and building interactive SCORM/xAPI packages.
*   **Pricing Approach:** Often priced per developed "learning hour" or as a comprehensive project fee depending on media requirements (video editing, custom graphics, voiceovers).

### 3. Corporate AI Ethics & Literacy Training
*   **Scope:** Developing custom training modules based on a company's internal AI policies.
*   **Pricing Approach:** Custom scoped engagements.

## Retainers and Ongoing Support
For businesses that require continuous course updates, technical LMS administration, or ongoing instructional design support, we offer monthly retainer models. This provides a dedicated "virtual e-learning team" without the overhead of full-time hires.

## Getting a Quote
Because e-learning projects vary wildly in complexity (e.g., a simple video upload vs. a highly interactive branching scenario), we do not publish standard pricing tiers.

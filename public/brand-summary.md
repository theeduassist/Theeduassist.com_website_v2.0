# TheEduAssist Brand Summary

## Company Identity
TheEduAssist is a specialized e-learning design and course-building agency. We help creators, educators, coaches, consultants, training businesses, online academies, and corporate teams turn their expertise into structured, launch-ready online learning systems.

## Brand Tone & Voice
*   **Professional & Clear:** We avoid overly technical jargon unless necessary, prioritizing clear communication that business owners and HR directors understand.
*   **Helpful & Confident:** We guide our clients through complex technical and instructional design decisions with expertise.
*   **Honest & Transparent:** We do not make fake claims about legal compliance, revenue guarantees, or fake platform partnerships.

## Visual Identity
*   **Logo:** The official transparent brand logo is `public/brand/theeduassist-logo-transparent.png`.
*   **Alternative:** An optimized circular variant is `public/brand/theeduassist-logo-circle.png`.
*   **Alt Text Rule:** Always use the explicit alt text "TheEduAssist e-learning design agency logo".
*   **Display:** Ensure aspect ratios are maintained. Use `rounded-full` CSS wrappers for the circular display.

## Operating Model
TheEduAssist operates as a remote/global agency. We work with clients worldwide across North America, Europe, the Middle East, Asia Pacific, and more. We explicitly do not claim to have physical local offices in the cities we service.

## Core Value Proposition
We bridge the gap between raw subject matter expertise (PDFs, slides, ideas, workshops) and a polished, technically sound Learning Management System (LMS) or course platform. We handle the instructional design, the technical setup, and the content migration.

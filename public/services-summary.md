# TheEduAssist Services Summary

## Core Offerings

TheEduAssist provides comprehensive support across the entire e-learning lifecycle.

### 1. Kajabi Course Setup & Services
*   **Target:** Creators, Coaches, Solo-entrepreneurs.
*   **Description:** End-to-end Kajabi support, including pipeline/funnel creation, website design, course upload/cleanup, email automation, and migration from other platforms (like Teachable or WordPress) to Kajabi.
*   **URL:** `https://theeduassist.com/kajabi-services/`

### 2. Custom eLearning Development
*   **Target:** Training businesses, Corporate Academies.
*   **Description:** Transforming raw content (PDFs, slides, videos) into structured, interactive online courses. Includes instructional design, storyboarding, assessment creation, and SCORM/xAPI packaging.
*   **URL:** `https://theeduassist.com/services/custom-elearning-development/`

### 3. LMS Implementation & Migration
*   **Target:** Corporate L&D, Enterprise, Academic institutions.
*   **Description:** Strategic selection, technical setup, and secure migration of user data and courses to a new Learning Management System.
*   **URL:** `https://theeduassist.com/services/lms-implementation-migration/`

### 4. AI-Powered eLearning
*   **Target:** Forward-thinking training departments.
*   **Description:** Utilizing AI safely to accelerate course planning, structure content, and enhance training workflows, always with mandatory human review.
*   **URL:** `https://theeduassist.com/services/ai-powered-elearning/`

### 5. AI Ethics & Corporate Training
*   **Target:** Corporate Compliance, HR Leaders.
*   **Description:** Developing custom "AI Literacy" and "Responsible AI Training" programs to help employees understand safe and ethical AI usage at work. *Note: We provide educational training, not legal compliance guarantees.*
*   **URL:** `https://theeduassist.com/services/ai-ethics-corporate-training/`

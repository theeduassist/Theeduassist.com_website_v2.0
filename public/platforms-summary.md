# TheEduAssist Platforms Summary

TheEduAssist is a platform-agnostic e-learning agency. We match the right technology to our clients' specific business models and audience needs.

## Course & Coaching Platforms (Creator Economy)
Best for solo-entrepreneurs, coaches, and digital product creators focused on marketing and sales funnels.
*   **Kajabi** (Primary focus for creator services)
*   **Thinkific**
*   **LearnWorlds**
*   **Teachable**
*   **Podia**

## Community-Centric Platforms
Best for cohort-based learning and membership sites emphasizing peer interaction.
*   **Skool**
*   **Circle**
*   **Mighty Networks**

## Corporate & Enterprise LMS
Best for internal employee training, compliance tracking, and B2B extended enterprise learning.
*   **TalentLMS**
*   **Docebo**
*   **Absorb LMS**
*   **LearnUpon**
*   **Litmos**
*   **Cornerstone Learning**

## Academic & Education LMS
Best for universities, schools, and formal accredited education programs.
*   **Moodle**
*   **Canvas**
*   **Blackboard**
*   **Brightspace**

## WordPress LMS Plugins
Best for businesses that want complete control over their tech stack and self-hosting.
*   **LearnDash**
*   **Tutor LMS**

## Authoring Tools & Standards
Used for building interactive, portable course packages independent of the delivery platform.
*   **Articulate 360 (Storyline & Rise)**
*   **SCORM 1.2 / SCORM 2004**
*   **xAPI (Experience API)**

*Note: TheEduAssist is not an "Official Partner" of these platforms unless explicitly stated with verified badges on our website. We provide expert technical and instructional support for them.*

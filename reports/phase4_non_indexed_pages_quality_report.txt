# Phase 4 - Non-Indexed Page Quality Report

## Audit of Search Console Issue Groups
**Target Groups:** 'Crawled - currently not indexed', 'Discovered - currently not indexed'

### 1. Tag/Category Archive Pages (e.g., `/blog/tag/...`, `/blog/category/...`)
*   **Issues:** Thin content, largely duplicate content (list of excerpts), often weak title/meta.
*   **Decision:** **Keep noindex**. These are navigational aids and typically do not possess enough unique value to compete in SERPs. Do not force into sitemap.

### 2. Pagination Pages (e.g., `/blog/page/2/`, `/blog/page/3/`)
*   **Issues:** Duplicate intent, lower unique value compared to the root `/blog/` page.
*   **Decision:** **Keep noindex** (or canonicalize to the root if content is highly similar, but standard practice often leans towards noindex, follow for deep pagination to save crawl budget for actual posts). Ensure they remain out of the XML sitemap.

### 3. Thin Location Pages (e.g., smaller tier 3 cities if they exist)
*   **Issues:** Duplicate city/location text (boilerplate copy just swapping city names), weak internal links, not enough unique value.
*   **Decision:** **Merge/Redirect** or **Keep noindex**. Only tier 1 and indexable tier 2 locations should be indexable. If a location page lacks comprehensive, unique content passing quality gates, default to 'noindex'. Ensure they are removed from the sitemap (already handled by `generate-sitemap.mjs` checking `c.indexStatus === 'index'`).

### 4. Legacy Service Pages with Low Traffic
*   **Issues:** Old template/design, weak CTA, thin content, missing H1, weak internal links.
*   **Decision:** **Improve and keep indexable** (if the service is still core) OR **Merge/Redirect** (into a broader, stronger service page). Manual review required for specific pages falling into this bucket in GSC.

### 5. Drafts / Review Pending Pages (if accidentally discovered)
*   **Issues:** Unfinished content, missing schema, weak internal links.
*   **Decision:** **Keep noindex**. Remove from sitemap immediately (already handled by `generate-sitemap.mjs`).

## Actionable Next Steps
1.  **Do not force weak pages into the sitemap.** The current `scripts/generate-sitemap.mjs` strictly filters out `noindex`, `hidden`, and `reviewPending` pages, which is correct.
2.  **Only useful canonical pages should be indexable.** Continue to rely on page-level `noindex` meta tags rather than `robots.txt` disallows for managing these non-indexed pages.
3.  **Manual Review:** The site owner must review the exact URLs listed under 'Crawled - currently not indexed' in GSC and apply the decision matrix above.

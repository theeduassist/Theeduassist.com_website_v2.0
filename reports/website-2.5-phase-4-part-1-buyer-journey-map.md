# Enterprise Buyer Journey Map

## Primary Buyer Roles
- Chief Learning Officer
- Head of Learning and Development
- L&D Manager
- HR or People Development Leader
- Talent Development Leader
- Training Manager
- Customer Education Leader
- Customer Success Leader
- Sales Enablement Leader
- Partner Enablement Leader
- Product Education Leader
- Operations Leader
- Digital Learning Manager
- LMS Administrator
- Learning Technology Leader
- Training-provider leadership
- Association or academy leadership

## Decision Stages
1. Recognize the Problem
2. Explore Options
3. Evaluate Capability
4. Prepare Internal Approval
5. Start the Engagement

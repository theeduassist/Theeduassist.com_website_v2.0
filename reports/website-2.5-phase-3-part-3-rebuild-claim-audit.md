# Claim Audit Report: Phase 3 Part 3 Rebuild

For every risky claim identified in Part 3 services, the following controls were implemented:

- **Zero-data-loss migration:** Removed. Explicitly stated under Limitations on the LMS page: "Zero data loss cannot be guaranteed due to variations in legacy exports."
- **Universal platform compatibility:** Removed. LMS page explicitly states "Compatibility requires testing" and "Some content or specialized configurations may need manual recreation."
- **Guaranteed SCORM or xAPI tracking:** Removed. "Tracking accuracy and reporting depth can vary significantly between platforms."
- **Guaranteed WCAG compliance:** Removed. QA page clearly states: "We check against accessibility best practices... but we do not provide legal accessibility certification or guarantee absolute compliance."
- **Guaranteed AI accuracy:** Removed. AI page clearly outlines rigorous human review needs and states: "AI output can be inaccurate or misleading without human oversight."
- **Automated course quality:** Removed. AI page states: "AI does not guarantee improved learning outcomes automatically."
- **Guaranteed productivity gains:** Removed. AI page states: "We do not guarantee exact productivity improvements."
- **24/7 support:** Removed. Support page states: "24/7 availability or emergency out-of-hours support is not assumed." Managed services page states: "24/7 support is not assumed."
- **Unlimited revisions:** Removed. Support page states: "Unlimited revisions are not assumed in any support model."
